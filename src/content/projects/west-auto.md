WestAuto is a web platform that presents a clear and structured catalog of cars, allowing users to browse available vehicles through an organized interface with clean navigation and well‑designed detail pages. The project focuses on clarity, usability, and a smooth viewing experience, making it easy for visitors to explore different models and find the information they need.
